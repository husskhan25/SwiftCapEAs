Common Questions

Contact FAQ

What is the fastest way to get support?

Our Discord server is the fastest channel — responses typically within minutes during market hours. The development team and community are both active and ready to help with setup, troubleshooting, and optimisation.

How long do email replies take?

We respond to all emails within 24 hours on business days. For urgent issues — especially during live trading — we recommend Discord for real-time assistance rather than email.

Can I request a custom EA or modification?

We consider custom development requests on a case-by-case basis. Please describe your requirements using the contact form above with "Partnership / Affiliate" as the subject, and our team will evaluate feasibility.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Use the contact form with "Bug Report" selected as the subject. Include your EA name, MetaTrader version, broker, and a detailed description of the issue. Screenshots or journal logs are extremely helpful for diagnosis.

Do you offer affiliate or partnership programmes?

Yes. We have affiliate partnerships for brokers, VPS providers, and content creators. If you're interested in collaborating, reach out via the form above or contact us directly at swiftcapeas@gmail.com.

Where can I leave a review?

Product reviews can be left directly on our MQL5 Market seller page. Your feedback helps other traders make informed decisions and helps us improve our products.
