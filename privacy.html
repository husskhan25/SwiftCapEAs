Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (hashed).
  • Payment Information: Processed entirely by Whop — we do NOT store credit card numbers.
  • License Data: License keys, activated trading account numbers, hardware identifiers.
  • Usage Data: API requests including timestamps and IP addresses.
  • Communication Data: Emails and Discord messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage accounts and licenses
  • To deliver products and license keys
  • To validate licenses
  • To send transactional emails
  • To provide customer support
  • To prevent fraud and license abuse

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT sell personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT share trading account data
  • We do NOT access trading accounts or broker credentials
  • We do NOT track trading activity, balances, or positions
  • We do NOT send marketing emails unless you opt in

4. Third-Party Services

  • Whop — payment processing
  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Vercel — website and API hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

5. Data Security

  • Encrypted passwords (bcrypt hashing)
  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure API authentication tokens
  • HMAC signature verification on webhooks

6. Data Retention

We retain account data while the account is active. Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. To request deletion, email team@swiftcapeas.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, request correction, request deletion, and withdraw consent for optional communications. To exercise any of these rights, email team@swiftcapeas.com.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
