Common Questions

EA FAQ

Which EA is best for prop firm challenges?

Both the Master EA and Breakout EA are purpose-built for prop firm environments. The Breakout EA leads on monthly returns (+12.4%) while the Master EA has the highest win rate (85%). Trinity EA is also prop firm compatible for advanced users.

Do I need MT4 or MT5?

Master EA and Breakout EA work on both MT4 and MT5. Grid EA and Trinity EA are MT5 only. We recommend MT5 for the most features and best performance across all our systems.

How do I get the EAs after purchase?

All EAs are licensed through MQL5 Market. After purchase, they appear in your MetaTrader platform automatically — no manual file transfers needed.

Can I run multiple EAs simultaneously?

Yes. Many traders run Master EA and Breakout EA simultaneously on different instruments for diversified exposure. We recommend a minimum balance of $500 per EA for optimal performance. Join our Discord for multi-EA setup guides.

Why is Grid EA not prop firm compatible?

Grid and DCA strategies stack multiple positions as price moves against the trade. This can breach prop firm drawdown rules during adverse market conditions. Grid EA is optimised exclusively for personal funded accounts with appropriate risk tolerance.

What broker do you recommend?

We recommend IC Markets or FP Markets for tight spreads and fast execution — critical for scalping strategies like Master EA. Both are ECN brokers with raw spread accounts.

Is a VPS required?

A VPS is strongly recommended to ensure 24/5 uptime without interruption. We partner with ForexVPS for reliable, low-latency hosting optimised for MetaTrader. Setup guides are available in our Discord.

Where can I verify live performance?

All live results are published on MyFXBook with third-party verification. Real-time trade alerts and performance updates are also shared in our Discord community.
