EA FAQ

01 Which EA should I use for prop firm challenges?

The Breakout EA and Master EA are both engineered for prop firm compliance with strict daily drawdown caps. The Trinity EA is also prop firm compatible. The Grid EA is the only one not recommended for prop firms due to its higher drawdown profile.

02 Do the EAs work on MT4 as well as MT5?

The Master EA and Breakout EA are available for both MT4 and MT5. The Grid EA and Trinity EA are MT5-only, as they leverage MT5-specific features for their multi-position and multi-layer logic.
